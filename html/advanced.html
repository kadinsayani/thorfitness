Advanced Program

The Advanced Program focuses on maintaining strength while adding isolation exercises to make further muscle gains.

Workout Frequency Workout Length Equipment Needed
5 days per week 45-60 minutes Barbell, Dumbbells, Bench, Squat Rack, Cable Machine, Hammer Strength Machines, Pull up/Dip Bar

An example schedule could be lifting Monday, Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday, and Friday, and resting on the weekends.
