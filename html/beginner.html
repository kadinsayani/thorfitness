Beginner Program

The Beginner Program focuses on building foundational strength while maximizing muscle gains.

Workout Frequency Workout Length Equipment Needed
3 days per week 45-60 minutes Barbell, Dumbbells, Bench, Squat Rack

During the Begginer Program, it is important to prioritize rest. Rest allows your muscles and central nervous system to recover, so you can continue to make progress and crush workouts in the gym. An example schedule could be lifting on Monday, Wednesday, and Friday.
