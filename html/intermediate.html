Intermediate Program

The Intermediate Program focuses on developing strength and power while stile emphasizing hypertrophy. The program is split up into two upper body days and two lower body days, allowing for more focus on lower body development. Training legs stimulates a hormonal response which will aid in developing muscle mass and strength. The program also features training days focused on strength and power, and training days focused on hypertrophy.

Workout Frequency Workout Length Equipment Needed
4 days per week 45-60 minutes Barbell, Dumbbells, Bench, Squat Rack

An example scedule could be lifting on Monday and Tuesday, resting Wednesday, and then lifting again on Thursday and Friday, allowing for rest on the weekends.
